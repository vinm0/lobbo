Build a Lobby

Lobbies bring people together for a common purpose. Organize lobby members to meet or collaborate. Lobbies are ideal for planning events and coordinating with lobby members.

Collaborate with teams with custom lobbies. Get together to plan, party, or play. Invite friends to join your lobby and make magic happen.

Join Lobbies

Contribute to projects, make new experiences, share your expertise. Lobby members make the whole lobby work. Whether you're meeting new people or making plans with old colleagues, what you bring to the table matters.

Track Progress

Set goals, measure metrics, assign leader roles. Let your ambitions run wild! Or just plan a regular meet-up. Defining your lobby's purpose will help you meet your goals.
